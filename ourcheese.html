Our specialist cheeses

Artisan Cheeses

We here at Cheezebo are beyond passionate about great quality, artisan cheese. See below just a small sample of these amazing cheeses.

We work closely with many cheese producers across the UK and Europe to ensure supreme quality and choice. We will also work closely with you, our customers, ensuring the cheese we supply on your big day will be your particular favourites.

We can taylor our packages to suit you and will include all other products to compliment, such as crackers, biscuits, fruit and chutneys.

Brie

Our brie comes from the Seine-et-Marne region of France, famous for its Brie. Along with the cows milk they add extra cream and traditional methods to mature it, giving it a beautifully soft and buttery taste. Winner of multiple world food awards and only one of many Brie cheeses we offer.

Cheddar

Our cheddar is simply gorge-ous! Thats right, why go anywhere else. Our independent small artisan cheesemaker we use have also won accolades around the globe. The only producer left in the village of Cheddar, Somerset makes this cheese unique. Its crumbley, very mature and has a tasty crunch.

Stilton

Cropwell Bishop Stilton has a rich tangy favour, a velvety-soft texture that melts in your mouth. Hand-crafted using methods that havent changed since the 17th century. A absolute must for any stinky blue cheese lovers!

Gorgonzola

This beautiful piccante Italian blue cheese comes from the Lombardy region, where the natural caves are used traditionally in the aging of it. Strong, sharp and oh so very pungent and spicy aroma makes this particularly memorable. We love it here at Cheezebo, especially paired with some ripe figs. Bellissimo!
