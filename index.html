About us

We here at Cheezebo are the cheese gazebo specialists. No one knows their cheese and their gazebos, like we know them!

We can cater for all tastes, whether subtle and mild or honking and strong! We're not only extremely knowledgable but also use our great network of suppliers, offering the best, tastiest cheese from all over Europe.

Though based in Kent we offer our catering services throughout the UK. We travel far and wide in our refridgerated cheese-mobile and can often be found serving up at all weddings, birthdays and major music festivals, up and down the country!
